##Metal Engineering Works- Job, Client & Materials Management System
A user realistic small business oriented information system in which a small metal fabricating business make use of a web based interface utilizing modern web technologies to handle jobs, customers and materials.
##Project Overview
This system assists a metal engineering workshop in digitalizing and streamlining their work, drawing the paper records and spreadsheets and utilizing a functional and easy to use tool to:

Tracking and status of jobs Job tracking and status of jobs

Management of relations with clients Client relations management

Inventory and cost control of materials
##Job Management
The jobs undertaken in track fabrication should be tracked according to the client selected, the description, material used, cost and job status (pending, working on it and finished).

Block status rollback and negative costs.

##Client Management
Attend to contact information: name, responsible person, phone, email, address.

Deleting of clients with outstanding jobs.

##Material Inventory
Add/edit/delete materials that include their quantity, unit, cost per unit, supplier details.

Avoid destruction of supplies used.

Automatically-compute stock value.
##Dashboard
Get a reading of total jobs, active jobs and total clients.

##Technical Stack
Layer Technology
Frontend HTML5, pure JavaScript
Backend Node.js, Express.js (REST API)
Storage Data JSON files (jobs.json, clients.json, materials.json)
